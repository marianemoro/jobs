Photovoltaic Programm

The campaign was launched among the employees of the company to try to understand the costumer profile, who would buy the photovoltaic system.

My work in this project was create the illustrations for all the campaign using the 3 types of costumers identified by the client and then we created 3 different slogan campaigns, but the illustration styles remained the same.

I carried out a large research project about the photovoltaic system and then I realised that an infographic could illustrate and communicate better about how it works.
