KP Outreach

The proposal of the KP Outreach system was to give pre-medical care to the patient through an online interface. The patient logs in and requests a service, meanwhile he basically describes the symptoms and, if necessary, sends photos of any area containing marks, for example. Soon after this step, the service with a health professional will begin.

For this interface design and prototype, we used the exploratory research and conversations with the client to understand the needs and a lot of tests with diferent versions of prototypes.

In this project my work was create the patient journey from the brief and translate it to the interface.
