GestCom App

This is an internal project at Leroy Merlin Brazil and it's to help the employees at the store with inventory and tag price control.

On this project:

User experience, user interface, mobile app

The problem

Leroy Merlin stores are huge and the vendors has so many responsabilities besides to sell product and help customers, they need to keep their area clean, organized, with a determinated produtc amount and the tag prices updated. When a customer has a price problem at the checkout, the vendor has to help them to make sure they have the right price, and this kind of issue can cause a big customers line.

With this scenario, we saw an opportunity to implement an app to help the employee's stores to have a better productivity.

The MVP

The project started as a MVP to test the poster print feature. At this time the employee needed a help from another collegue to print this poster, made with Excel spreadsheet, so it was a very complex task. This first feature was plugged in another price tool and we setup some paper formats, the current campaings and the employee just needed to type or scan a product code to search the price and name information to print out.

The next step

After this first feature and few tests. I went to talk with those employees to understand their most important tasks, their pains and as about their gains if they'd have more time. After to understand their scenario, I started to design a userflow, then I created a paper prototype to test with those people, then I designed a high fidelity prototype to do a second test before to send for development.

Results

This project was my first job for a physical store.

This project started very small, with just one feature and today it's the most important job tool for the store's employees, today they can control the inventory, price, they have some reports about their problems and they can plan how to act or how to prevent it.
