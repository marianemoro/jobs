Leroy Merlin Ecommerce

Omnichannel project through the ecommerce platform.

On this project:

User experience, user interface, web application

The challenges

The biggest challenge here was create a solution to the whole buying flow, including the product page, marketplace, order tracking and checkout. All of those features should be an omnichannel solution.

Product Page and Marketplace

After to analyze the product page numbers with Google Analytics and Hotjar, we decided to talk with the business area to start a new product page project, but first we called a meeting and asked the business team to fill a spreadsheet with references about what is an ideal product page, and what kind of features we should have there.

We've done more 2 meetings to ask different teams to help us to create the new structure of a new page, with the new features and components, so we've got a room, post-its and a board to draw it.

After this fase, I designed a wireframe and then a high fidelity prototype to test the new features with some customers, to understand if we'd need to do some changes or not. Then I went to the store to test it out and check some questions we had about the new features, amount of the information on the same screen and asked the users to do few tasks like choose a product color, calculate the shipment price and add to cart.

Omnichannel Order Tracking

In 2017 we launched the Assistant Sales system, a solution to improve the customer experience in our stores when they need a sales person to buy a product, with this process the customer can buy product with our help and selfservice products, but at this time we didn't have a order tracking to show the order tracking for physical store purchases. We were receiving a lot of call from our curstomers asking about their order status and delivery status.

This feature helps our customers and our employees to know about the order status from every buy channel, like ecommerce, physical store or sales from telephone. So our customers doesn't need to call us to ask about the order if they access our website.

The concept of this solution is split the different shippments whatever is the kind of sales, because at this time we were about to launch the marketplace project and the customer would have more than one shippment at least with this kind of buying flow.

This project was created with Supply Chain team, Call Center team and Assistant Sales team.
