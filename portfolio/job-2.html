Suppliers Portal

A project to improve the relationship between Leroy Merlin Brazil and suppliers.

On this project:

User experience, user interface, web application

The challenges

The project was created to improve the productivity and communication between the company and the suppliers.

For every new feature, we started with a value proposition design canvas and we're working with Agile Method to develop the application.

The value proposition canvas helped us to understand the user's tasks, pains and what they seen as a gain, it's a good interview tool guide when you have a new system to create.

The bills to pay module

The portal was build as a modular system, so the modules can work separated or together. Our first feature was the Bills to Pay module, before the employee had to do the bank consolidation and then send to the supplier, but they have a lot of other tasks at the company, so after this interview, I started to design a user flow for this solution, I tested the paper prototype with our keyuser and then I've tested the high fidelity prototype with the supplier, after those tests we started to develop our firts module. Today the supplier doesn't need to ask the employee to check about their bills.

The order schedule module

The second module was the order schedule on the warehouses and stores. The company hasn't a system to schedule the orders from the suppliers and they needed like 5 days to do this for each supplier, with the new solution the supplier can schedule in about 2 or 3 minutes without any help from the company and the employees can spend their time with other tasks.

The surprises

This project was my first with supply chain and the financial departments.

I've haver worked with a supply chain project before, so I had to learn a lot about logistics and their business rules. I had to learn about the suppliers reality and their pains too when a big company doesn't have a good solution to work with the suppliers and what kind of problem the final custmer can have, like a lack of products to buy, for example.

Today this project is a profit application, because the company has like different prices for any kind of service through the portal.
