SB Trade Branding

SB Trade is a foreign trade company. The communication aims to promote Brazil and mainly Florianopolis, because the city has an importance in the import sector due to its location, considered strategic to the business.

In this project I worked with all branding materials: logo, email marketing template and wordpress website.

During this process, after various meetings, we started to build an infographic to illustrate how the import and export processes work and the result of those graphics are available on the company's website.
