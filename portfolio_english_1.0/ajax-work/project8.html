Diabesity

This course won 2 contests at the USA, Articulate Guru Awards 2010 and e-Learning DemoFest 2010.

Diabesity course was created to start an awareness of the direct link between obesity and type 2 diabetes, the content was designed for the North American public.

The content has been developed from exploratory research, based on public health statistics North American.

The infographics and interactions were created to send as much information and as light as possible.
