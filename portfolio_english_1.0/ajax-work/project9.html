How to survive a big foot attack

The presentation was one of the winners of the e-Learning Demofest 2011.

    This presentation was a marketing strategy to launch the Keen shoes collection in the USA in 2011.

    The proposal was to teach the users what we need to do or not on a trail to prevent a wild animal attack, through this training the user can see the new shoes from the Keen collection.
