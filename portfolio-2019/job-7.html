KP Outreach

The proposal of the KP Outreach system was to give a pre-medical care to the patient through an online interface.

On this project:

Service Design, User Interface

The challenges

The patient logs in and requests a service, meanwhile the paitent basically describes the symptoms and, if necessary, sends photos of any area containing marks, for example. Soon after this step, the service with a health professional will begin.

For this interface design and prototype, we used the exploratory research and conversations with the company to understand their needs and a lot of proposals with diferent versions of prototypes.

The surprises

Patient journey and prototype

This project was my first service design project with an on-line sistem as an outcome. I had to create the patient journey from the brief and translate it to the interface.
