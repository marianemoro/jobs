Tractebel Energy

Tractebel is an energy generator and their biggest concern is about the environment, so the company decided to test the employees engagement about to use photovoltaic system and then decide how they could start to sell this system to the final customer.

On this project:

Marketing campaing, graphic design, MVP

The challenges

My work in this project was create the illustrations for all the campaign using the 3 types of costumers identified by the client and then we created 3 different slogan campaigns, but the illustration styles remained the same.

I carried out a large research project about the photovoltaic system and then I realised that an infographic could illustrate and communicate better about how it works.

The surprises

Branding and styleguide project.

After the first fase, where I worked with the complete flow and validate with the manager, I started to create a brand and a color sheme to have a foundation for a styleguide.
