How to survive a bigfoot attack

This presentation was a marketing strategy to launch the Keen shoes collection in the USA in 2011.

On this project:

User Interface, Development

The challenges

The company asked us to create an innovating campaing to launch their new shoes collection.

Our proposal was use this campaing to tell a story about the bigfoot and teach the customers what they need to do or not on a trail to prevent a wild animal attack, through this training the user can see the new shoes from the Keen collection.

The surprises

e-Learning Reward

The presentation was one of the winners of the e-Learning Demofest 2011.
